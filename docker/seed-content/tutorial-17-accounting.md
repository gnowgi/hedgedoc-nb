# Lesson 17 · Capstone — personal accounting

Knowledge graphs aren't only for science. A completely different domain shows how
general nodeBook is: **personal accounting**. A transaction *moves value* between
accounts — money flowing out of one account and into another is, structurally,
exactly a transition with prior and post states.

- **Accounts** (Assets, Liabilities, Income, Expenses) are nodes — classified
  with the schema ideas from Lesson 11.
- **Transactions** are transitions (Lesson 12): value flows from one account to
  another.
- **Balances** compute with Function-style evaluation (Lesson 14), and the ledger
  totals update as you go.

Explore the worked example:

- 💰 **[nodeBook ledger & transactions](/n/nodeBook_transactions)** — a personal
  ledger built entirely in CNL.

Same primitives, a world away from frogs and enzymes — that's the point.
